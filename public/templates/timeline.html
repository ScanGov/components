ScanGov

Timeline

A custom scangov.css component — not part of Bootstrap. Uses a left-border line with dot markers (:after pseudo-element) to connect a vertical sequence of steps. Each step is a card with a two-column interior: icon column (.col-auto, min-width 4rem) and content column (.col.border-start). The last item should omit .pb-3 to avoid extra space below the final dot.

Step timeline

Used for process flows like "How it works." Constrain width with col-md-8 or similar — full-width timelines look too spread out.

  • Scan

    We fully scan your website.

  • Tasks

    We create a prioritized tasklist of items to fix.

  • Fix

    Fix issues with your team, agency, or vendor.

  • Monitor

    We continuously monitor for new issues.

Changelog variant

For scan history or changelog entries use .timeline-domain and .timeline-date helper classes (both defined in scangov.css). No card wrapper needed — simpler list item content.
